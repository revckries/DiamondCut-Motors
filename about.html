About Us

Welcome to Diamond Cut, where automotive excellence meets unparalleled luxury. We are a premier destination for discerning enthusiasts who demand the finest in high-performance vehicles and bespoke craftsmanship.

  • We are located in Indonesia since 1998 and have selected 105 branches throughout Indonesia.
  • We have a license and official letter from the government to sell quality imported cars.
  • We fix 4 out of 5 cars at the roadside.

Vision

Leading in the luxury automotive market by consistently exceeding with offering an extraordinary selection of premium vehicles and deliver a personalized, customer-centric experience that sets new standards in the industry.

Mission

Curate a collection of the world’s most prestigious automobiles and provide a seamless, enjoyable purchasing process tailored to each individual’s needs.

History

Established in 1998, DiamondCut Motors was founded on a passion for luxury and a commitment to excellence. Our journey started with a simple idea to provide car enthusiasts with access to the finest vehicles in the world.
